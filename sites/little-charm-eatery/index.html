Little Charm Eatery brings the vibrant flavors of Thailand to Wallingford, CT. Every dish is prepared fresh to order, with authentic recipes elevated by modern presentation. From fragrant curries to perfectly balanced pad Thai, we take pride in generous portions and genuine hospitality.

Whether you're joining us for a leisurely dinner or grabbing takeout, we treat every guest like family. Walk-ins always welcome.
