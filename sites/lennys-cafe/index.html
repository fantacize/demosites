What We're About

01

Zuppardi's Pizza

Legendary New Haven-style personal pizzas served hot. The best bar food in town, and that's not up for debate.

02

Draft & Craft Beer

Cold ones on tap plus a full selection of bottles and cans. Your favorite is probably here. If not, we'll figure it out.

03

Live Sports

Multiple screens showing every game that matters. Football, basketball, baseball, hockey. Every seat's a good seat.

04

Full Bar

Classic cocktails made right. Shots, mixed drinks, and whatever your regular order is. We probably already know it.

05

Bar Food

Hot dogs, chicken tenders, and all the essentials. Simple food done right. You're here for the vibes anyway.

06

Darts & Good Times

Electronic BullShooter boards, great regulars, and an atmosphere money can't buy. This is the real deal.
